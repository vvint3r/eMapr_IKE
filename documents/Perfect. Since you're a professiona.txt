### 1. **Data Infrastructure & Governance**

* **Concepts**: Data sources (1P, 3P), event tracking, schema design, CDPs, pixel/tag management
* **Tools**: Segment, Tealium, GA4, Snowplow, RudderStack
* **Models**: Event taxonomy, schema governance, tracking plans
* **Metrics**: Data freshness, signal loss, consent rate
* **Responsibilities**:

  * Define tracking plans and naming conventions
  * Manage CDP/event pipelines
  * Audit data flows and signal integrity

---

### 2. **Measurement & Attribution**

* **Concepts**: Attribution windows, touchpoint modeling, last-click vs data-driven, incrementality
* **Models**:

  * **MTA**: Logistic regression, Shapley values, Markov chains
  * **MMM**: Bayesian/Elastic Net regression, Ridge, Hierarchical models
  * **Incrementality**: Geo-RCTs, Ghost Ads, CUPED
* **Metrics**:

  * Assisted conversions, lift %, cost-per-incremental
* **Responsibilities**:

  * Select appropriate attribution models
  * Run lift/incrementality tests
  * Present CAC efficiency and blended ROAS

---

### 3. **Channel & Campaign Analytics**

* **Concepts**: Full-funnel analysis, creative testing, conversion lag, channel overlap
* **Models**: ROAS optimization, media mix allocations, portfolio models
* **Metrics**:

  * CAC, CPA, ROAS, Conversion Rate (CVR)
  * Frequency, Reach, CPM, CTR
* **Responsibilities**:

  * Weekly channel pacing + media efficiency
  * Creative testing insights and flighting strategy
  * Budget recommendation modeling

---

### 4. **Product & Web Analytics**

* **Concepts**: Customer journey, engagement loops, behavioral funnels
* **Frameworks**: AARRR (Acquisition, Activation...), HEART (Google UX metrics)
* **Tools**: GA4, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Hotjar
* **Metrics**:

  * Bounce Rate, Page Load Time, Session Duration
  * DAU/WAU/MAU, Retention Cohorts
* **Responsibilities**:

  * Run A/B tests on landing pages or flows
  * Diagnose funnel issues
  * Analyze onsite behavior and optimize for UX/SEO

---

### 5. **Customer & Market Intelligence**

* **Concepts**: RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary), segmentation, CLV, churn risk, NPS
* **Models**:

  * CLV: Historical, predictive, probabilistic
  * Segmentation: k-means, HDBSCAN, behavioral clustering
* **Metrics**:

  * LTV/CAC ratio, churn rate, NPS, CSAT
* **Responsibilities**:

  * Build audience personas
  * Predict customer value
  * Analyze market share and competitive movement

---

### 6. **Experimentation & Causal Inference**

* **Concepts**: Randomized testing, CUPED, DiD, Instrumental Variables
* **Frameworks**: AA tests, A/B/n, Multivariate, Geo-RCTs
* **Metrics**:

  * Uplift, statistical power, MDE, p-value
* **Responsibilities**:

  * Design experiments with pre-registered hypotheses
  * Create statistical templates (e.g., CUPED corrections)
  * Interpret lift and significance of changes

-------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------
