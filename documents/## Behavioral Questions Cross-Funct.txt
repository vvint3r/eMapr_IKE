## Interview Preparation Tips

### STAR Method Framework
For each answer, structure your response using:
- **Situation:** Set the context
- **Task:** Explain the challenge or responsibility
- **Action:** Describe specific steps you took
- **Result:** Share measurable outcomes and learnings

### Key Themes to Emphasize
- **Quantifiable Impact:** Reference your $800M revenue generation at Adobe, $2.5M at Shopify, and team leadership experience
- **Technical + Business Balance:** Show how you bridge complex analytics with business pragmatism
- **Trust Building:** Demonstrate transparency about data limitations while maintaining confidence
- **Scalability:** Highlight your experience scaling processes across teams and organizations
