This is a comprehensive, structural breakdown of the Marketing Analytics landscape. It is designed as a curriculum or a master ontology, covering the discipline from high-level strategy down to granular statistical implementation.

The structure flows logically from **Foundations** $\rightarrow$ **Data Architecture** $\rightarrow$ **Methodologies (Math/Stats)** $\rightarrow$ **Execution (Channels/Tactics)** $\rightarrow$ **Synthesis (Reporting/Org).**

***
